MISSION

Our mission is to restore dignity to refugees through economic empowerment by connecting them to online work, allowing businesses to maximize their internal human potential while contributing to a more socially just and productive world.

VISION

We envision a world where talent knows no boundaries,
​opportunity doesn’t ask for status, and work is rewarded with fair pay.

VALUES

respect

Above all we at WorkAround believe that respect should not be tied to status. It doesn’t come with the labels or designation society hands out,  it’s earned through actions, commitment and follow through.​

quality

At WorkAround, we value quality just as much as you do.  We cultivate a culture of getting it right, which means empowering our workers to ask the right questions in order to deliver the right results.  We build in quality control measures through initial screenings, ongoing training, and self and peer checks.​

dignity

Being able to be support yourself and your family, and being proud of what you do to earn that support is something we believe in at WorkAround.  Because we value our employees and take pride in their work, we pay them fair wages.  This means we may not always be able to offer our customers the lowest price out there on the market, but we do offer the best value for your money.  Because our employees take pride in and are empowered by the opportunities for skill development on our platform, they are fiercely committed to quality.

growth

As a start-up, we understand the importance of learning from mistakes and using them to do better and grow bigger.  Our workers are encouraged to harness their own growth potential through our partnerships with free online Universities, where they can take courses to help them qualify to complete more complex tasks on our website.  We help our workers find employment during difficult times when flexibility is key and other options are closed, but we don’t want them to stay with us forever.  We want to help them grow their potential so when the time is right for them to seek local employment, they will be prepared with great skills and references.
